About Bossbby Tours & Travel

Our Story

Bossbby Tours & Travel was founded by Aisha Nandita, a teacher by profession with an unwavering zeal and passion for tourism. The name "Bossbby" was given to her by her first travelers - her coursemates at the University - who were amazed by the luxurious services delivered at very low prices.

What started as a passion project has now evolved into a premier travel company specializing in East African safari adventures. We combine luxury experiences with affordability, making once-in-a-lifetime adventures accessible to everyone.

Our founder's teaching background ensures that every trip is not just a vacation, but an educational experience that connects you deeply with the cultures, wildlife, and landscapes of Africa.

Our Services

Gorilla Trekking

Experience the thrill of encountering mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. Our expert guides will take you on an unforgettable journey through the forests of Uganda and Rwanda.

Chimpanzee Trekking

Get up close with our closest primate relatives in their natural environment. Our chimpanzee trekking adventures offer unique insights into primate behavior and conservation.

Cultural Tourism

Immerse yourself in the rich cultures of East Africa. Visit local communities, participate in traditional ceremonies, and learn about indigenous ways of life.

Customized Group Trips

We design personalized travel experiences for groups of all sizes. Whether it's a family reunion, corporate retreat, or friends' adventure, we create the perfect itinerary for you.
